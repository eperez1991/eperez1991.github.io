Education

My education begins on the wonderful island of Puerto Rico where I grew up. I attended the University of Puerto Rico at Rio Piedras straight out of high school with hopes of attaining a Bachelor in Natural Sciences. But I always had a passion for technology. Near the end of my freshmen year, the students went on strike and put my education on hold for 3 months. I decided that it would be in my best interest to move. In 2010, I made the move to my home town of Chicago. I started attending community college and working on my English. In 2012, I applied to the Illinois Institute of Technology to pursue a degree in Information Technology and Management.
